Vacation Days Calculator

Calculate your expected vacation days credit at year end

Usage

This tool calculates the number of vacation days credit an employee is expected to have at the end of the year, based on current accumulated hours, annual entitlement, and planned vacation consumption.

Insert your New Balance (יתרה חדשה) as it appears in your latest pay slip to calculate your expected excess vacation days at the end of the year.

Make sure that your Annual Allowance (מכסה שנתית) is correctly entered (either in days, or in hours - as it appears in the pay slip):

In the Extra Vacation Days Consumption field you should sum up vacation days which are not yet included in the latest pay slip. This consists of:

  • Any vacation days taken during the previous month ()
  • Any vacation days taken during the current month ()
  • Any planned vacation days expected to be taken from now on until the end of the year

The latest pay slip and extra vacation information are available on the Hilan site (make sure to update your attendance).

Note: Extra vacation days consumption must be in half-day increments (0, 0.5, 1, 1.5, 2, etc.) and cannot exceed the number of working days from the start of the previous month.

A work day is 8.417 hours.
